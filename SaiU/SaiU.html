THE DIVERSE SCHOOLS OF SAI UNIVERSITY

Sai University is renowned for its innovative academic structure, offering a unique interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary approach to education. As one of the first institutions in the country to introduce multidisciplinary model across diverse fields, Sai University is home to seven distinct schools. Each school is dedicated to fostering an environment where students can explore diverse fields, encouraging them to expand their horizons and engage in meaningful collaborative research and study.
